Will the SEC approve a spot Solana ETF in 2025?

listed 2024-06-01 · resolved 2025-12-31
Retrospective. If your team had been running Pred-Oracle while this contract was live, here's what you would have seen. Sources: [1][2]

Resolution criteria

Resolves YES if the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission approves at least one spot Solana exchange-traded fund (an ETF holding SOL tokens directly, not futures-based) for listing on a U.S. national securities exchange (NYSE Arca, Cboe BZX, or NASDAQ) with an effective approval order issued on or before 2025-12-31. Resolves NO otherwise.

Conditions for YES

  1. A
    SEC approves spot SOL ETF on NYSE Arca
    By 2025-12-31, SEC issues an effective order approving a NYSE Arca rule change to list and trade a spot Solana ETF (holding SOL directly, not futures).
  2. B
    SEC approves spot SOL ETF on Cboe BZX
    By 2025-12-31, SEC issues an effective order approving a Cboe BZX rule change to list and trade a spot Solana ETF (holding SOL directly, not futures).
  3. C
    SEC approves spot SOL ETF on NASDAQ
    By 2025-12-31, SEC issues an effective order approving a NASDAQ rule change to list and trade a spot Solana ETF (holding SOL directly, not futures).
