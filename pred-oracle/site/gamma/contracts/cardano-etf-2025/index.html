Resolution criteria

Resolves YES if the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission approves a spot Cardano (ADA) ETF for trading on a national securities exchange by December 31, 2025, 11:59 PM ET. Resolves NO otherwise.

Conditions for YES

  1. A
    19b-4 approval for ADA ETF listing
    By Dec 31, 2025, the SEC issues an order approving an exchange’s Rule 19b-4 to list and trade shares of a spot Cardano (ADA) ETF on a U.S. national securities exchange.
  2. B
    ADA ETF starts trading on exchange
    By Dec 31, 2025, shares of a spot Cardano (ADA) ETF actually list and commence trading on any U.S. national securities exchange, implying SEC approval for trading.
