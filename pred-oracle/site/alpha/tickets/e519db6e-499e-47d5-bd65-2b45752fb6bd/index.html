CFTC Sues Minnesota to Block State Law

View primary source

Published2026-05-19
Effective2026-08-01
Typeenforcement / regulatory_body
JurisdictionsUS, MN, AZ, CT, IL, NY, MA

What changed

The CFTC filed a lawsuit seeking to prevent Minnesota's law from taking effect on August 1, 2026, which would criminalize prediction market activities as felonies.

Why it matters

This update highlights federal efforts to maintain regulatory supremacy over prediction markets and protect lawful market participants from state-level criminalization.

Key requirements

  • Businesses must be aware of the pending legal challenge and potential injunction preventing enforcement of the Minnesota law.

Penalties / consequences

  • Criminal felony charges for operating or assisting prediction markets under Minnesota law if enforced; reputational and financial risks for affected businesses.
