California Department of Justice Releases Proposed "Protecting Our Kids from Social Media Addiction Act (SB 976)” Regulations - 14.05.2026

View primary source

Published2026-05-14
Comment deadline2026-06-30
Typeproposed rule / regulatory_body
JurisdictionsUS-CA

What changed

Proposed regulations require age assurance and parental consent mechanisms on social media platforms targeting minors, with a public comment period and hearing to solicit feedback.

Why it matters

This update addresses growing concerns about social media addiction among minors and imposes regulatory obligations on platforms to mitigate these risks.

Key requirements

  • Adopt age assurance and parental consent processes as per the Act.
  • Solicit and consider public comments on nondiscrimination impacts.
  • Participate in public hearings if desired.

Penalties / consequences

  • Potential legal enforcement actions by California DOJ for non-compliance.
  • Reputational damage due to failure to protect minors as required by law.
