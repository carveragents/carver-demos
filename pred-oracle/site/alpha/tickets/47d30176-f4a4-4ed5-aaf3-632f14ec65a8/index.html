What changed

David H. Goldman is suspended from association with any broker, dealer, investment adviser, municipal securities dealer, municipal advisor, transfer agent, or nationally recognized statistical rating organization for twelve months effective immediately.

Why it matters

This update enforces accountability and deters unregistered securities sales, protecting investors and maintaining market integrity.

Key requirements

  • Immediate suspension from association with regulated entities for twelve months.

Penalties / consequences

  • Twelve-month suspension from association with broker-dealers and related entities
  • Permanent injunction from future violations of securities laws
  • Reputational damage and potential financial consequences
