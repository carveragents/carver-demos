CFTC Sues Wisconsin to Reaffirm its Exclusive Jurisdiction Over Prediction Markets

View primary source

Published2026-04-28
Typeenforcement / enforcement_agency
JurisdictionsUS-WI, US-NY, US-AZ, US-CT, US-IL, US-MA

What changed

The CFTC filed a lawsuit against Wisconsin to block state lawsuits against certain CFTC-regulated prediction markets, reinforcing federal preemption over state gambling laws in this area.

Why it matters

This update highlights ongoing jurisdictional conflicts between federal and state authorities over regulation of prediction markets, posing legal and operational risks for affected businesses.

Key requirements

  • Businesses must comply with federal regulation under the CFTC and may face legal challenges from states attempting to assert jurisdiction.

Penalties / consequences

  • Legal consequences including lawsuits and injunctions; reputational risk from public legal disputes; potential operational disruptions.
