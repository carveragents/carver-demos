Resolution criteria

This market will resolve to 'Yes' if marijuana is rescheduled down from Schedule I to any other level (or is unscheduled completely) by the Drug Enforcement Agency of the United States by December 31, 2026, 11:59 PM ET. Otherwise, this market will resolve to 'No'. The primary resolution source will be official information from the U.S. government, however a consensus of credible reporting will also be used. Note: In April 2026, FDA-approved marijuana products and state-licensed medical cannabis were moved to Schedule III under President Trump's executive order. This market specifically tracks whether recreational marijuana (currently Schedule I) is rescheduled. If the DEA reschedules only specific cannabinoids or derivatives without reclassifying marijuana as a whole, that does not qualify.
