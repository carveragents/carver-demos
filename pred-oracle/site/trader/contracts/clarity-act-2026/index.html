Resolution criteria

This market will resolve to 'Yes' if the Digital Asset Market Clarity Act of 2025 (H.R.3633) is passed by both chambers of the U.S. Congress and signed into law by December 31, 2026, 11:59 PM ET. Otherwise, this market will resolve to 'No'. The primary resolution source will be official information from Congress.gov, the White House, and the U.S. Government Publishing Office. The CLARITY Act (also referred to as the Digital Asset Market Clarity Act) establishes a regulatory framework distinguishing digital commodities from digital securities, granting the CFTC primary jurisdiction over digital commodity spot markets and clarifying the SEC's jurisdiction over digital asset securities.
