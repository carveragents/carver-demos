How it works

  1. 1 For each contract, Carver decomposes the resolution criteria into named conditions and the regulatory bodies whose actions could resolve them.
  2. 2 Every regulatory filing Carver ingests is scored against those conditions — directly relevant (↑↓), tangential background, or unrelated.
  3. 3 You get a tier label (critical/active/watch/dormant), a percentile rank vs your other positions, and a timeline of every event that contributed.

Demo data: portfolio and prices are real (sourced from Polymarket and Kalshi APIs); positions are illustrative. Regulatory intelligence is generated from Carver's live corpus. Not investment advice.
